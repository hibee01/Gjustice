THE PROJECT

GJustice TeleLegal Limited is a technological Service Company, that connects subscribers to enlisted licensed Legal Practitioners through the Gjustice Service platform and Offline Tech Points, which allows the public request for Legal Services. With G-Justice, Users can get all crimes and injustices promptly reported and attended to by the vast group of lawyers available on the platform, no matter who or where they are. Users can also ask legal questions and receive responses from qualified attorneys.

SUMMARY OF USE CASE

There are 3 levels of Users Classification:

  • The Subscriber (they needs legal services)
  • The lawyer or Legal Firm
  • Administrators (who monitor and assign tasks from offline channels)

THE CHALLENGE

One of the biggest challenges for this project was providing a unique experience to an entire customer base on free service and a premium paid service plans. This meant the design needs to balance the business objectives, get users to opt into a premium service, and also provide browsing users with the opportunity to find more general legal help. Another challenge was creating legal options for different types of users in one experience.

THE GOAL

  • Make it easy for for users to connect to Legal Practitioners
  • Design a safe environment that makes users feel comfortable asking their legal questions
  • Make it easy for users to ask questions and receive answers from qualified legal Practitioners
  • Manage cases and update case history
  • Design a platform where subscribers can launch a campaign for other users to donate to makea a case possible
  • Read daily legal news and articles
  • Create a blog where subscribers and legal Practitioners can have discussions
  • Design a safe environment that makes users feel comfortable asking their legal questions
  • Create opportunities that lead freemium users upgrade to premium subscriptions
